FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What Minecraft versions are supported?
We support all versions from 1.8 to 1.20+. Our server uses ViaVersion to ensure compatibility across all Minecraft versions.
Is there a whitelist? How do I join?
No whitelist! Simply connect to play.fallenhalo.net and you're ready to start your adventure immediately.
Does the server have land claiming?
No. There are no land claim plugins. Players are responsible for protecting their builds through smart base locations, alliances, and security.
Are there donation ranks?
Yes, but they are purely cosmetic. No pay-to-win advantages are sold on our store.
How can I report a player?
Use /report in-game or open a ticket on our Discord server for staff assistance.
Are there world resets?
World resets are kept to a minimum. We aim to preserve player progress and long-term projects whenever possible.
